What is Gumboda Productions?

Every filmmaker amateur dreams of having their own production company to make their movies look much more professional. That’s where Gumboda Productions comes in. What started as a joke, became more serious with every project. Now the team is working on projects bigger than comedies or documentarys ...and the journey only just began.

Featured Project(s)
Nest 17 - Alone.

General Information:
Name: Nest 17 - Alone
Genre: Shortfilm, Zombie, Apocalypse, Survival
Release Year: 2026
Planned Duration: 15min
Description: Years after the outbreak, the viewer follows a lone survivor through a zombie apocalypse.

Production:
Alone will be part of the Nest 17 movie universe, which is our largest project so far. This is the first time we go through the whole filmmaking process. By using After Effects, DOF (depth of field) and color grading, we improve the quality of each shot to make this short film even more cinematic.
